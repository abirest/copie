About

We are an Accounting, Management and Finance Consulting firm created in January 2023 by its founder Abir Ben El Haj. We support our clients, mainly financial institutions (banks and international development agencies, commercial banks, Islamic banks, microfinance institutions , leasing companies, etc.), in the success of their strategic, development and organizational projects, from design to implementation, by ensuring their management and their change management component.
We also work with regulatory and supervisory authorities and public bodies for studies and missions to support the development of the financial sector and the financial inclusion of small entrepreneurs.
We intervene both within the framework of technical assistance programs financed by banks and international development agencies, as well as within the framework of missions contracted directly with our financial institution clients.
